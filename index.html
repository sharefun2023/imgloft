FAQ

How does imglite convert SVG to PNG?

imglite uses your browser's built-in Canvas API. The SVG is loaded into an image element, drawn onto a canvas, and exported as a PNG — all client-side.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Your files never leave your device. We have no server-side processing, no logging, and no way to see your images.

Can I resize the PNG output?

Yes. Enter custom width and height before converting. Leave them blank to keep the original SVG dimensions.

Is there a file size limit?

No artificial limits — only what your browser can handle. Most SVGs convert instantly.

What about batch conversion?

Batch processing is coming soon. For now, convert one SVG at a time — each conversion takes less than a second.
