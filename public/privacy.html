Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

1. The Short Version

imglite processes all images entirely in your browser. We never see your files. We never upload your images to any server. We don't use tracking cookies. We don't collect personal data.

2. How Image Processing Works

All image conversion, resizing, and compression happens client-side using your browser's Canvas API and JavaScript. Your files stay on your device at all times. imglite has no server-side image processing — there is nothing to upload to.

3. Data We Collect

We don't collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No images — your files are never uploaded, seen, or stored by us.
  • No accounts — there is no signup or login.
  • No tracking cookies — we don't use analytics or advertising trackers.
  • No logs — we don't maintain server-side logs of visitors or usage.

4. Third-Party Services

imglite is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect basic technical data (IP addresses, browser type) for security and CDN delivery purposes, as outlined in Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies

imglite does not set any cookies. The Cloudflare CDN may set a __cf_bm cookie for bot protection — this is a technical necessity and does not track you.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, create an issue on our GitHub repository.
