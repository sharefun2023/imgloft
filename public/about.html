About imglite

What is imglite?

imglite is a collection of free online image tools that run entirely in your browser. No uploads, no signups, no watermarks, no limits. Just fast, private image processing.

Why imglite?

Most online image tools upload your files to a server. That's slow. That's a privacy risk. And it usually comes with limits. imglite does everything client-side — your files never leave your device. Ever.

Features

SVG to PNG conversion — Convert vector graphics to raster PNG instantly. Set custom dimensions and quality.

Image resizing — Resize images to exact dimensions without uploading. Keep aspect ratio or set custom sizes.

Privacy first — All processing is client-side. No server uploads. No tracking. No cookies beyond what's technically necessary.

Who made this?

imglite is built and maintained by a solo developer who believes simple tools should be free, fast, and respect your privacy. No VC backing. No data harvesting. Just useful tools.
